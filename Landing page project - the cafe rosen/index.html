Our History


Welcome to the captivating story of Cafe Rosen, where rich tradition meets modern charm. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, Cafe Rosen has been an integral part of the community, serving up not just coffee but a warm, inviting ambiance that has become a cherished backdrop for countless memories.Named after the enchanting rose gardens that once graced the area, Cafe Rosen has always been synonymous with beauty and elegance. The founders envisioned a place where patrons could escape the hustle and bustle of daily life, immersing themselves in an atmosphere of tranquility and joy.Cafe Rosen has always been more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee. We take pride in being an integral part of the community, hosting events, supporting local artists, and providing a welcoming space for friends and family to gather. The walls of Cafe Rosen echo with laughter, conversations, and the clinking of cups a true reflection of the bonds forged within. Cafe Rosen has witnessed numerous milestones and celebrated countless special moments with our patrons.we are honored to have been a part of your stories.

Menu

1. Our Signature Coffees

Rose Gold Latte

A velvety blend of espresso, steamed milk, and a hint of rose flavor, topped with delicate rose petals.Indulge in the enchanting allure of our Rose Gold Latte a meticulously crafted coffee masterpiece. This velvety elixir seamlessly blends the robust notes of espresso with steamed milk,creating a harmonious balance. The subtle infusion of rose flavor adds a delicate touch, while the finishing flourish of rose petals elevates the experience.

Caramel Macchiato

Our Caramel Macchiato is a delightful dance of flavors. This classic coffee creation features a perfect harmony of rich espresso, velvety steamed milk, and a hint of vanilla syrup, all crowned with a luscious drizzle of caramel. Each sip is a journey through layers of sweetness and warmth,making the Caramel Macchiato a cherished choice for those seeking a delectable blend of indulgence and sophistication. Elevate your coffee experience with this sweet symphony.

Espresso Affogato

Immerse yourself in the indulgent simplicity of our Espresso Affogato. This exquisite treat begins with a shot of robust espresso, poured gracefully over a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream. The marriage of intense coffee and velvety sweetness creates a luxurious contrast, awakening your senses with every spoonful. It is not just a dessert; it's an experience a perfect union of bold flavors and delightful textures that will leave you craving the symphony of espresso and ice cream perfection.
